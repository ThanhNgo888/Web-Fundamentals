World Port Shrimp

Treat yourself to an amazing selection of sumptuous shrimp dishes from Omaha Steaks. From superb appetizers like our Cooked Peel and Eat Shrimp to tantalizing entrees like Shrimp Fettuccine Alfredo, you'll find a smorgasbord of seafood fare that is unequaled.

Nutrition & Preparation Instructions

Preparation: Do Not Thaw!


Oven: Preheat oven to 400°F. Place frozen tray on baking sheet in center of oven with film intact. Bake 30 minutes. Carefully peel back film; stir. Re-cover with film. Bake an additional 10 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Let stand 5 minutes

Microwave: Place frozen tray in microwave with film intact. Heat on high for 6 minutes. Carefully peel back film; stir. Re-cover with film. Microwave an additional 2-3 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
